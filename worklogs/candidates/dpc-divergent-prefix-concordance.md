DPC (Divergent-Prefix Concordance) maintains a sign-vote tensor `V[s_div, a, a', m]` indexed by trajectory-pair first-divergence events: for each pair in the buffer that first diverges at state `s_div` with actions `a` vs `a'`, each channel `m` accumulates `sign((c_T(τ_i) − c_T(τ_j))[m])`. The improvement operator fires a logit nudge at `s_div` toward actions with per-channel majority margin that is also Pareto-non-dominated by the reverse — no scalar collapse, no Bellman backup, no critic, no bucket-coverage precondition (every trajectory pair in the buffer has a first-divergence event).
