## Reasoning

CRP's central primitive — within-trajectory rank-percentile of channel firing magnitude, restricted to the channel's own firing-set — is structurally distinct from every disqualifier family. The hypothesis's own nearest-disqualifier audit names GVFs/successor features and makes the key distinction precise: successor features integrate channel *magnitude* over future steps (`E[Σ_t γ^t φ(s_t)]`), whereas CRP's primitive is a rank-position statistic that is unitless, magnitude-invariant, and non-additive across trajectories. Under variable renaming this distinction holds: rank-percentile cannot be expressed as a discounted sum of any feature map φ because it requires the full per-trajectory firing-set (a global-within-episode statistic), not a cumulation. The closest prior alive candidate, VCC, is explicitly differentiated as a magnitude-integral / Pareto-continuation-front statistic; the closest failed family, FED/CEC/CEC descendants, are differentiated as hash-collision-gated multi-trajectory comparison primitives, whereas CRP fires from a single completed trajectory. The structural-distinction paragraph holds under scrutiny. All required-candidate-shape slots are filled with substantive answers: experience object, core primitive, improvement operator (Pareto logit nudge on trend-corrected rank vector R̃), execution rule, vector feedback rule (channels kept separate throughout, no scalarization, Pareto comparison law), rollout-cost discipline (one rollout per episode, seeding phase counted in budget), four concrete predicted failure modes, side-information channel (vector diagnostics + coarse observation-hash), and a monotonic improvement claim naming a concrete potential Φ and the condition under which it increases.

The improvement operator must also survive the cross-attempt "terminal-only channel collapse" ruling that killed CHX (#17) and PICAV (#16). CRP's response is honest: on a terminal-only channel that fires once at the last step, rank-percentile is constant 1.0, the R cell degenerates, and falsifier (a) checks for this explicitly. The operator degrades to "this channel carries no rank signal" rather than collapsing to a magnitude-integral rebadge — the degenerate case is flagged as a predicted failure mode on Deep Sea Treasure, not hidden. This does not reduce to any disqualifier; it is a real limitation with a named check.

## Risks the Engineer should be aware of

- The seeding phase (N_seed random episodes before the operator fires) shares the same structural shape as the bootstrap wall that killed FED (#15) and its descendants (#18–#21). CRP claims escape because rank-percentile can be computed from a single completed trajectory without requiring cross-trajectory hash collisions; this claim is plausible but unverified — verify that R cells are actually populated and the Pareto nudge fires within the 120 s budget on the sparse envs before concluding the bootstrap wall is truly bypassed.
- The observation-hash clustering is declared as "transition geometry" in the side-information channel. If collision rate is high (coarse clusters) the rank-percentile means average across genuinely different states; if too fine, cells are singletons and Pareto comparison is unreliable. The Engineer should instrument cluster cardinality and R-cell coverage as a diagnostic.
- On monotone-progress envs (DeepSea-like), rank-percentile correlates strongly with step-index; the hypothesis flags this as a predicted failure mode and proposes step-index-conditional detrending as a mitigation. The mitigation is not yet specified precisely enough to implement unambiguously — the Engineer should decide on a concrete bucketing scheme before running.
